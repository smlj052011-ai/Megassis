How to find your UID

You need your Unique ID (UID) to provide feedback and access account features.

  1. 1. Open the Megassis.Client app.
  2. 2. Go to the "Account" or "Settings" tab.
  3. 3. Locate the field labeled "Device Identity UID".
  4. 4. Tap to copy and use it in our feedback forms.
